About Me

High School Student

My 19-year life journey has been shaped by a diverse mix of cultures. Born and raised in Vietnam, I was deeply rooted in its vibrant traditions and values. At the age of 12, I moved to Korea, and this transformative experience has had a profound impact on my perspective and personal development. Living and experiencing both Vietnamese and Korean societies has given me a unique vantage point on the world. Growing up in such a dynamic, multicultural environment has nurtured in me the ability to adapt, accept diverse perspectives, and appreciate the nuances of different cultures. These experiences have developed me into a flexible and open-minded person who always eager to learn and grow. Inspired by my multicultural background, I am driven by a passion for learning and discovery. I am currently passionate about learning about the world of coding and technology, where I eagerly look forward to using my creativity and problem-solving skills to make a positive contribution. My diverse experiences fuel my curiosity and motivate me to continually expand my knowledge. As I continue to learn and grow, I’m committed to using my experiences to make meaningful contributions to the world around me. Whether through projects or personal growth, I strive to combine the insights gained from my multicultural journey with my passion for technology and innovation.
